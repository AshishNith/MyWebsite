My Projects

Line Follower Bot

 Nov 2023

A line follower robot is an autonomous robot that uses sensors to detect and follow a line on the floor. It typically includes a chassis, sensors (like infrared or optical), a microcontroller (such as an Arduino), motors, and a power supply. The sensors detect the line and send signals to the microcontroller, which adjusts the motor speeds to keep the robot on course. Line follower robots are used in education for teaching robotics and programming, in industry for automated guided vehicles, and in robotics competitions.

Obstacle Avoidance Bot

 Sep 2023

Developed an autonomous robot designed to navigate around obstacles using sensors. The robot comprises a chassis, distance sensors (ultrasonic or infrared), a microcontroller (Arduino), motors, and a power supply. The sensors measure distances to obstacles and send signals to the microcontroller, which adjusts motor speeds and direction to avoid collisions. This robot is used in educational settings for teaching robotics and programming, in industrial applications for automated navigation, and in robotics competitions.
